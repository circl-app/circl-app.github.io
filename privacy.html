Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Circl ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Circl mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Phone number — used for contact matching and account identification
  • Full name — displayed to other users
  • Username — your unique identifier within the App
  • Profile picture — optionally uploaded by you

1.2 Device Contacts

With your explicit permission, we access your device's contact list (names, phone numbers, and email addresses) to help you find people you know on Circl. Contact data is synced to our servers and stored securely. You can revoke contact access at any time through your device settings.

1.3 Content You Create

We store content you create within the App, including:

  • Questions you ask
  • Responses you submit
  • Comments and reactions
  • Media attachments (images, videos, audio)
  • Lists and groups you create or join

1.4 Device Information

We collect your device's push notification token to deliver notifications. We also collect basic device information (platform type: iOS or Android) for service functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Match your contacts to existing Circl users
  • Deliver questions, responses, and notifications
  • Send SMS invitations on your behalf (see Section 3)
  • Store and serve media you upload
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. SMS Messaging Program

Circl offers an SMS invite feature that allows users to send question invitations to contacts who are not yet on the App.

3.1 Consent

SMS messages are sent only when a Circl user explicitly chooses to invite a contact to respond to a specific question. The inviting user initiates each message. By using the SMS invite feature, you (the sender) confirm that you have a prior relationship with the recipient and believe they would want to receive the invitation.

3.2 Message Content and Frequency

Each SMS contains the sender's name, the question text, and a link to respond. Recipients will receive a maximum of 2 SMS messages per 6-month period. Senders are limited to 10 SMS invitations per day. Message and data rates may apply.

3.3 Opting Out

Recipients can opt out of future SMS messages by replying STOP to any message received from Circl. You may also contact us at circlproject2026@gmail.com to request removal from our SMS recipient list. Once opted out, you will receive no further SMS messages from Circl.

3.4 Help

For help with SMS messages, reply HELP to any message or contact us at circlproject2026@gmail.com.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Database, authentication, serverless functions All account and content data
Twilio SMS delivery Recipient phone number, message content
Expo Push notifications Device tokens, notification content
Cloudinary Media storage and delivery Images, videos, and audio you upload
Google OAuth sign-in (optional) Email, name, profile picture (if you choose Google sign-in)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • Questions, responses, and comments — retained until you delete your account or the content creator deletes the question
  • Device contacts — retained until you delete your account
  • Device tokens — retained while your app is installed and active; deactivated on sign-out
  • Notification logs — retained for 24 hours for delivery retry purposes
  • SMS invite records — retained for rate-limiting and referral attribution

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App. When you delete your account, we permanently delete:

  • Your profile information
  • All questions you created
  • All responses, comments, and reactions you submitted
  • Your contact list data
  • Your device tokens and notification preferences
  • Your friendships, lists, and groups
  • Your media attachments

Anonymized invite attribution records may be retained for referral tracking after account deletion.

7. Data Security

We implement row-level security on all database tables, ensuring users can only access data they are authorized to view. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Circl is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at circlproject2026@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at circlproject2026@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
